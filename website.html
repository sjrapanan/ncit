Neo Culture
Institute of Technology

Neo Culture Institute of Technology (NCIT) is a
South Korean-based university specializing in engineering courses.
Founded in 2021 by Lee Soo-Man of SM Entertainment.
NCIT is known to offer quality education in various fields such as
engineering, business, and arts. NCIT now opens its doors for aspiring
freshmens for Batch 2028.
